About Me

I'm a chemical engineer interested in developing computational chemistry tools using machine learning and quantum chemistry. I'm currently working as a postdoctoral scholar at the University of Chicago with Prof. Andrew Ferguson (@Ferglab). I was a graduate student at UIUC working with Prof. Peters (@Peterslab). I have worked on developing the importance learning algorithm, an active learning algorithm for calculating site-averaged kinetics of amorphous catalysts. I have also developed multiscale models for studying reactions in series and effects of reaction kinetics on crystal growth.
